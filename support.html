1. Welcome to BMIGo Support

Thank you for using BMIGo. Our goal is to help you easily calculate, track, and monitor your Body Mass Index (BMI) and health progress.

If you experience any issues, have questions about the application, subscriptions, Apple Health integration, or would like to provide feedback, we're here to help.

2. How BMIGo Works

BMIGo is a personal BMI tracking application designed to help users monitor their body measurements and health progress.

The application allows you to:

  • Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Record your weight and height
  • Save BMI history
  • View progress with charts and statistics
  • Track long-term BMI trends
  • Optionally synchronize supported data with Apple Health
  • Manage subscriptions and premium features

3. Common Issues and Solutions

  • BMI Records Missing
    Ensure that your records have been saved successfully and that you are using the same device where the data was originally stored.
  • Apple Health Not Syncing
    Verify that BMIGo has permission to access Apple Health by opening the Health app and reviewing Health Access settings.
  • Statistics Not Updating
    Check that valid weight and height measurements have been entered. Updating or adding new records will automatically refresh your statistics.
  • Subscription Not Recognized
    Use the "Restore Purchases" option within the application to synchronize your subscription status.
  • Application Performance Issues
    Restart the application and make sure you are using the latest available version.

4. Apple Health Support

BMIGo supports optional integration with Apple Health.

If Health synchronization is not working:

  • Ensure Apple Health permissions have been granted.
  • Verify that Health data is available in the Apple Health app.
  • Disable and re-enable Health permissions if synchronization stops unexpectedly.
  • Restart BMIGo after changing Health permissions.

Health access can be managed at any time through the Apple Health application.

5. Data Storage

BMIGo stores your BMI records, weight history, height information, notes, and statistics locally on your device.

The application does not upload your personal health records to our servers.

If data appears missing after deleting the application or changing devices, previously stored local records may no longer be available unless restored from your own device backup.

6. Subscriptions and Purchases

BMIGo offers optional premium features through subscriptions.

All purchases are securely processed through Apple In-App Purchases and subscription management is handled by RevenueCat.

If premium features are unavailable despite an active subscription, please use the "Restore Purchases" option within the application.

If the issue continues, please contact support and include your User ID together with a description of the problem.
