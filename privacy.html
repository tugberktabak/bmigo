Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

1. Information Collection

BMIGo is designed with a privacy-first approach. The application does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

To support subscription management and application functionality, BMIGo generates a random anonymous user identifier (UUID). This identifier is randomly generated, is not linked to your identity, and cannot be used to personally identify you.

Users may voluntarily enter personal health-related information into the application, including body weight, height, Body Mass Index (BMI) records, notes, and other measurements used to monitor personal progress.

2. How BMIGo Works

BMIGo is a personal Body Mass Index tracking application that allows users to:

  • Calculate Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Record weight, height, and BMI measurements
  • Track historical BMI changes over time
  • View progress through statistics and visual charts
  • Monitor personal health trends
  • Optionally synchronize supported health data with Apple Health

BMIGo is intended solely as a personal tracking tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

3. Data Storage

All personal data entered into BMIGo is stored locally on your device.

Your BMI history, weight records, height information, notes, statistics, and other health-related information remain under your control and are not uploaded to our servers.

BMIGo does not maintain cloud databases containing your health records and does not use your personal data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or behavioral analysis.

4. Apple Health Integration

BMIGo optionally integrates with Apple Health (HealthKit). Health access is completely optional and requires your explicit permission through Apple's permission system.

If permission is granted, BMIGo may read and/or write supported health data such as:

  • Height
  • Body Weight
  • Body Mass Index (BMI)

Health data accessed through Apple Health is used exclusively to provide features requested by you, including synchronization, historical tracking, and progress visualization.

BMIGo does not use HealthKit data for advertising, analytics, marketing, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the application's health tracking functionality.

HealthKit information is never sold or shared with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

BMIGo uses a limited number of third-party services strictly necessary to provide subscription functionality.

  • RevenueCat — subscription management and purchase validation
  • Apple In-App Purchases — payment processing for subscriptions and premium features

BMIGo does not use advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, behavioral tracking services, marketing SDKs, or third-party advertising networks.

6. Payments and Subscription Information

All purchases are securely processed through Apple In-App Purchases.

Subscription status and premium entitlement management are provided by RevenueCat.

BMIGo does not collect, process, or store your payment card information, banking information, or billing details.

7. Data Security

Since your health records are stored locally on your device, you remain in control of your information.

Security of locally stored information depends in part on the security settings of your device. We recommend enabling device passcodes, Face ID, or Touch ID where available.

While reasonable measures are taken to design the application securely, no method of electronic storage can guarantee absolute security.

8. User Rights

You have full control over the information stored within BMIGo.

You may:

  • Add, edit, or delete BMI records
  • Disconnect Apple Health access at any time through Apple Health settings
  • Delete the application and all locally stored data from your device
  • Manage subscription status through your Apple ID account settings

9. Children's Privacy

BMIGo is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Medical Disclaimer

BMIGo is intended for informational and personal tracking purposes only.

The application does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding any medical concerns or health-related decisions.

Any calculations, statistics, charts, or BMI classifications provided by BMIGo should not be interpreted as medical guidance.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements to the application, legal requirements, or operational changes.

Continued use of BMIGo following publication of updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
